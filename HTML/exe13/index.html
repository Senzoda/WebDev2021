Pfizer vaccine: why a third dose of serum might be necessary

The emergence of new variants of the coronavirus is raising many concerns, particularly regarding the effectiveness of vaccines. According to Pfizer and BioNTech laboratories, their vaccine is 95% effective against the classic strain of the virus. But a study by the Institut Pasteur found that this serum protects 80% of the British variant and 60% of the South African variant. Currently, two doses of the Pfizer / BioNTech vaccine can provide immunity to patients.

Neymar pays the salaries of 142 Brazilian employees

Even though he earns around 37 million a year, Neymar still forgets where he comes from. While the “Instituto Projeto Neymar”, which he created to provide a better life for 3,000 underprivileged children in a Brazilian slum where he grew up, has been closed for 13 months due to the coronavirus, the PSG star continues to pay the salaries of the 142 employees who work there. "Jobs, wages and insurance are still standing, however long this crisis lasts," Neymar’s father said. All salaries represent a total of 90,000 euros per month, paid for more than a year by the Brazilian, who more than likely makes his employees happy in these difficult times.

If they can't afford to do that, the lender will sell the shares to try to recover what it is owed to the bank.
